'''[[IPv6|Internet Protocol Version 6]]''' ('''IPv6''') is not yet universally available as of 2012, but support by major [[ISP]]s and [[transit provider]]s is steadily increasing.  Many major transit providers offer an IPv6 service to their customers, but do not have a ubiquitous view of all other IPv6 networks.  Other aspects of this service vary widely from one major ISP to the next.
