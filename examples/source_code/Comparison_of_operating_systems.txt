These tables provide a '''comparison of operating systems''', listing general and technical information for a number of widely used and currently available [[personal computer|PC]] and handheld (including [[smartphone]] and [[tablet computer]]) [[operating system]]s. The article, [[usage share of operating systems]] provides a broader, and more general, comparison of operating systems that includes [[Server (computing)|servers]], [[Mainframe computer|mainframes]] and [[supercomputers]].

Because of the large number and variety of available [[Linux distribution]]s, they are all grouped under a single entry; see [[comparison of Linux distributions]] for a detailed comparison. There are also a variety of BSD operating systems, covered in [[comparison of BSD operating systems]]. For information on views of each operating system, see [[operating system advocacy]].
