These are lists of [[Wikipedia:Notability|'''''notable''''']] [[antivirus software|antivirus]] and [[Internet Security]] [[software]] in the form of comparison tables, according to their platform (e.g. [[Desktop computer|desktop]] and [[Server (computing)|server]], [[Mobile device|mobile]], etc.) and their [[operating system]]s (e.g. [[Microsoft Windows]], [[OS X]], [[Linux]], [[Oracle Solaris]], [[Google Android]], [[Apple iOS]], [[Windows Phone]], etc.).

The term "Email security" refers to the protection of emails from [[Computer virus|viruses]] and [[malware]], whilst "[[Antispam]]" refers to the protection from spam, scam and phishing attacks.
"Web protection" usually includes "identity protection" (privacy), "AntiPhishing" and "Online Banking protection".
